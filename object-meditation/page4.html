"It's Just Running"

"It's just running" - famous last words by yours truly.

We warmed up

by doing stretches.

A few neck rolls here

ankle rolls there,

back cracks there,

and then, we were started running

and continued to run

and run some more.

I was feeling pretty confident after

being able to keep up with

the rest of the team as

we jogged to a stop

My glee was shortlived.

"Great warmup," said my coach.

Ooookay. Pause.

You're telling me.

That mile. Was a warmup.

A warmup.

Needless to say

the rest of the first

practice was hell.

Never in my life

had the hours

passed slower.

My palms were sweaty.

Knees weak.

Arms were heavy.

Then finally.

Finally, I could

see the end.

Just a

few more

steps

to

go

.

.

.
