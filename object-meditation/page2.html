Once upon a freshman year...

It was my first year in high school and I thought joining a sports team would be a great way to make some new friends.

The sports offered in the fall were:

    Soccer
    Volleyball
    Field Hockey
    Cross Country

As a rather uncoordinated person, I determined that my best bet would be cross country.

After all, how hard could just running be?

There would be no dangerous balls hurling at my face. Just me, prancing through the grass, being one with nature, like

Bambi.

It'd be so much fun.
