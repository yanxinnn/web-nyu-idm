Once upon a freshman year...

'Twas

my first

year in high

school and I

thought that joining

a sports team would be

a great way to make some

new friends. The sports offered

by my high school in the fall included:

soccer, volleyball, field hockey, cross country.

As a rather uncoordinated person, I determined that

my best bet would be cross country. After all, how hard

could just running be? There would be no dangerous balls

hurling at my face. Just me, prancing through the grass

being one with nature, just like Bambi. It'd be so

much fun. Oh, was I in for a surprise.
